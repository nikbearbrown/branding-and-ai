- RED: the "real job identified" stage marker — the reframe that turns failure into design traction, the one beat the reader must catch.

A working audit tool.
- Spatial: three equal columns side by side with a header definition and one worked example claim each; a footer rule stating "majority Needs-Evidence = hypothesis, not artifact."
- RED: the "Needs Evidence" column — the status that, when it dominates, means the persona is fiction; the reader's primary watch-point.
