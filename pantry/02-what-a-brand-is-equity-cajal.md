- Spatial: left node "Identity (sender)" → arrow across a labeled "GAP" → right node "Image (receiver)"; below, both feed a "Equity (value)" node; gap callout in the middle.
- RED: the "GAP" element — the place equity leaks and where brand work happens, the focal point.
