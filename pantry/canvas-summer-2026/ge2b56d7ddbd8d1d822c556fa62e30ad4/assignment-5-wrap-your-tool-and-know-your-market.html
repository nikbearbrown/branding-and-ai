Quick Tips 

Competitive Analysis: 

  • Actually try competitor tools or watch detailed demos 
  • Screenshot the real interface, not marketing pages 
  • Find reviews on Product Hunt, G2, or Reddit for user feedback 

Trademark Research: 

  • Search keyword COMBINATIONS, not just single words 
  • You're getting a sense of the landscape, not doing legal clearance 
  • This informs smarter naming in Assignment 6 

Wrapping Your A4 Workflow: 

  • You're not rebuilding - you're adding a user-friendly front end 
  • Connect to your existing n8n workflow, API, or Python functions 
  • Focus on making YOUR workflow accessible, not starting from scratch 
  • Clean, functional design using framework defaults (branding comes in A6) 

User Testing: 

  • Don't explain anything beforehand 
  • Confusion is data - watch where they struggle 
  • Capture their exact words for branding insights
