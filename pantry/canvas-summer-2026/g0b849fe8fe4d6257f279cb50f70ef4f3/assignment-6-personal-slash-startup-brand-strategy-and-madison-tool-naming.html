Use the class Figma board as a reference for structure and content: INFO7375 Branding & AI Figma Board

🎯 Assignment Steps

1️⃣ Develop Your Professional Brand Strategy (30 points)

First, choose ONE of the following:

  • Option A: Personal Brand (focused on you as a professional)
  • Option B: Startup Brand (based on your Madison AI tool)

Part A: Brand Foundation Elements (15 points)

Your Task: 🔹 Clearly articulate your chosen brand's foundation:

  • Mission (What): Your mission statement defines your core purpose—what you do, who you serve, and how you create value.
  • Purpose (Why): Your purpose statement is your deeper motivation—why you do what you do beyond making money or gaining recognition.
  • Vision (Where): Your vision statement describes your ultimate goal, where you want to go and the impact you want to have on the world.
  • Values (How): Your values guide your decisions and actions. Choose 3-5 values that represent what you stand for.

🔹 Create a Unique Value Proposition (UVP):

  • For Personal Brand: "I help [specific employers/clients] achieve [specific result] through [unique skills]"
  • For Startup Brand: "Our product helps [specific audience] achieve [specific result] through [unique offering]"
  • Document 3-5 iterations of your UVP with refinements
  • Provide final UVP with justification

💡 AI Enhancement: Use AI tools to refine your brand foundation elements for clarity and impact

Deliverable: 📍 Complete brand foundation document in PDF with UVP development process

Part B: Professional Positioning & Differentiation (15 points)

Your Task: 🔹 Define 3-5 brand pillars for your personal/startup brand:

  • Brand Promise: What employers/clients can expect from you
  • Brand Personality: 3-5 traits that define your professional character
  • Brand Story: Brief narrative connecting your background to your target audience's needs
  • Brand Differentiation: Key skills/features that set you apart in the job market
  • Brand Experience: How people feel when working with you/your product

🔹 Conduct a professional positioning analysis:

  • For Personal Brand: Analyze 3-5 professionals in your target field
  • For Startup Brand: Analyze 3-5 competitors in your market space
  • Create a competitive matrix comparing key strengths and approaches
  • Highlight your unique position and competitive advantage

💡 Example AI Prompt: "Help me develop professional brand pillars for a [your field] specialist"

Deliverable: 📍 Comprehensive brand pillars document with professional positioning analysis

2️⃣ Madison Tool Name Development & Validation (30 points)

Part A: Name Generation (10 points)

Your Task: 🔹 Generate 10 potential names for your Madison AI tool:

  • 5 descriptive names that clearly communicate functionality
  • 5 creative/abstract names that are distinctive and memorable

🔹 For each name, provide:

  • Brief rationale (2-3 sentences)
  • Category (descriptive or creative)
  • Initial impression score (1-10)

💡 AI Enhancement: Use AI tools to generate name options based on your tool's functionality

Deliverable: 📍 List of 10 names with complete documentation in your PDF

Part B: Trademark Search (10 points)

Your Task: 🔹 Conduct a trademark search for your top 5 name candidates using Justis.com https://trademarks.justia.com/🔹 Document for each name:

  • Search process (screenshots)
  • Results summary
  • Potential conflicts
  • Risk assessment (high/medium/low)

🔹 Narrow your list to 3 names based on trademark availability

Deliverable: 📍 Trademark search documentation with clear findings

Part C: URL Availability Search (10 points)

Your Task: 🔹 Research domain availability for your top 3 names:

  • Check .com, .ai, .io, and .app domains
  • Document available options
  • Assess domain quality and memorability
  • Consider alternative spellings if needed

🔹 Select your final name based on both trademark and URL availability 🔹 Provide final recommendation with complete justification

Deliverable: 📍 URL search documentation and final name recommendation

3️⃣ Professional Portfolio Analysis (20 points)

Your Task: 🔹 Research 5-8 professional portfolios in your target field:

  • For Personal Brand: Focus on portfolios of professionals in your desired role
  • For Startup Brand: Focus on founder/startup team portfolios

🔹 For each portfolio, analyze:

  • Overall presentation and structure
  • Project showcasing approach
  • Personal/professional storytelling
  • Technical demonstration methods
  • Unique elements that make it stand out

🔹 For each example, clearly articulate:

  • Specific elements you admire (with screenshots)
  • Why these elements are effective
  • How they could inspire your own portfolio
  • One thing you would improve or change

🔹 Create a "best practices" synthesis from your analysis

💡 Example AI Prompt: "Help me analyze these portfolio examples for a [your field] professional"

Deliverable: 📍 Complete portfolio analysis with specific examples and best practices synthesis
