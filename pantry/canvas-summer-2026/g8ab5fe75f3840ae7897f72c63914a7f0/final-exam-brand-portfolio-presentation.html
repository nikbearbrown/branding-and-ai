Overview

This is the culmination of everything. Not a summary of what you learned — a demonstration of what you can do. You'll present a complete, coherent brand system that proves you can integrate strategy, visual identity, storytelling, AI tools, and digital execution into something that works in the real world.

Every major agency and brand consultancy evaluates work by asking one question: would this hold up in front of a client? That's the standard here.

Choose one path (same path you've maintained throughout the semester):

  • Path A: Personal Professional Brand
  • Path B: Your Madison AI Tool as a Startup Brand

Presentation Format

10 minutes. Not 10 minutes and 30 seconds.

Practice until you can do it in 9:30. Points are deducted for going over.

Presentation Structure

  1. Brand Introduction (1 minute)
  • Brand name and positioning statement
  • One-slide brand essence
  • Path B: Tool name + single sentence describing what it does
  1. Brand Strategy (2 minutes)
  • Target audience, UVP, brand pillars
  • Competitive positioning
  • Path B: How your tool is different from other AI and branding tools
  1. Visual Identity Showcase (2 minutes)
  • Logo variations, color palette, typography
  • Website walkthrough (live or screenshots)
  • Social media examples
  • Path B: How brand identity extends into the tool's interface
  1. Brand Storytelling (2 minutes)
  • Present one of your brand stories in full
  • Name the narrative framework and briefly explain why you chose it
  • Connect the story to your brand values
  • Path B: Focus on the problem your tool solves and who it solves it for
  1. Madison AI Tool Demo (3 minutes)
  • Live demonstration — show it working
  • Highlight 2–3 key features
  • Explain the methodology powering it
  • Path A: How it could be part of a personal branding practice
  • Path B: Make the value proposition tangible in real time

Pro structure to follow: Objective → Audience → Positioning → Strategy → Execution → Demo
