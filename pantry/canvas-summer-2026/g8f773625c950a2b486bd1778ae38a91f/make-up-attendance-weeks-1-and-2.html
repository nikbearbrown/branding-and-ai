This assignment is for students who joined the course late. Students who were present on both week 1 and week 2 need NOT attempt this assignment. Completing this assignment will allow you to receive attendance credit for the week(s) you missed.

 

Instructions

Please complete all of the following:

  1. Watch the lecture recording(s):

    • Watch both Week 1 and Week 2 recordings if you missed both

    • Watch Lecture 1 only if you attended Week 2

  2. Submit a written report:

    • Write at least 1 full page per week missed

    • If you missed both Week 1 and Week 2, combine your work into one document, clearly separated by week

    • Your report should summarize key concepts, takeaways, and reflections from the lecture(s)

  3. Guest Speaker Requirement (Week 2 only):

    • If you missed Week 2, your report must also include notes from the guest speaker session with Enrique Sallent

 

Submission Guidelines

  • File type: PDF or DOCX

  • Submission: Online upload to Canvas

  • One combined document if you missed both weeks
