📅 Module 4 Overview
Total Points: 300

Module Objectives

Develop a comprehensive digital brand presence through AI-powered visual content creation, strategic LinkedIn optimization, and professional portfolio website development. Students will create cohesive visual identities, engage professionally on social media, and build functional portfolio websites that showcase their expertise.

Learning Objectives

✅ Create and refine visual narratives using AI-powered tools
✅ Align imagery and content with brand identity for professional branding
✅ Optimize LinkedIn profiles and create engaging social media content
✅ Build and deploy professional portfolio websites using modern platforms
✅ Integrate storytelling frameworks into digital content strategy
✅ Engage actively in professional networking and thought leadership

Week 9: AI-Enhanced Personal Branding Assignment (Total: 100 Points)

1️⃣ AI-Generated Visuals for Branding (45 points)

🔹 AI Image Generation (25 points)

  • Use AI tools (MidJourney, DALL·E, Canva AI, Adobe Firefly, Stable Diffusion, or Gradio) to generate three brand-aligned images
  • Images should represent your expertise, industry, or personal branding
  • Deliverable: Submit three AI-generated images with a 150-word reflection on brand alignment

🔹 Refining Visual Content with AI (20 points)

  • Generate multiple versions of an image and refine them based on feedback
  • Deliverable: Submit original AI-generated version alongside final refined version, explaining key changes made

2️⃣ Social Media Visual Branding (35 points)

🔹 LinkedIn Profile Image & Banner Optimization (20 points)

  • Create or refine LinkedIn profile picture and banner using AI-generated visuals
  • Use tools like Canva, Adobe Express, or MidJourney for cohesive LinkedIn brand presence
  • Deliverable: Submit before-and-after comparison of LinkedIn visuals with explanation of improvements

🔹 Social Media Thought-Leadership Post (15 points)

  • Share AI-generated visual on LinkedIn with caption (100-150 words) explaining relevance to personal brand
  • Engage with at least three industry-related posts by commenting or sharing insights
  • Deliverable: Submit screenshot of LinkedIn post and engagement (likes, comments, shares)

3️⃣ Class Participation & Engagement (20 points)

🔹 Active Class Participation (20 points)

  • Ask at least one question during class discussions or guest speaker sessions
  • Engage in class chat by contributing insights or responding to classmates
  • Connect with peers or guest speakers through follow-up discussions or LinkedIn interactions
  • Deliverable: Submit summary of participation, including questions asked or responses shared in class

Week 10: LinkedIn Optimization & Content Creation (Total: 100 Points)

LinkedIn Profile & Banner Design (30 points)

Update Your LinkedIn Profile (20 points)

  • Headline & About Section (10 points): Use UVP and Brand Mission to write compelling headline and About section (minimum 150 words), incorporating storytelling arc from previous modules
  • Complete Key Profile Sections (10 points): Professional profile photo, Experience & Education sections, list 5+ relevant skills

Create Custom LinkedIn Banner (10 points)

  • Use Canva, Adobe Express, or similar tools to create branded LinkedIn banner
  • Banner should visually communicate expertise (name, tagline, key skills, career focus)
  • Deliverable: Submit LinkedIn profile link and screenshot of profile with custom banner

LinkedIn Engagement & Social Media Post Creation (30 points)

Create Original LinkedIn Post with Visual (15 points)

  • Write 3-5 sentence post using storytelling arc to structure content
  • Topics: career journey, lessons learned, expertise development
  • Design custom LinkedIn post graphic using Canva or Adobe Express

Engage with Professionals (15 points)

  • Comment meaningfully on 3 posts from industry leaders, peers, or mentors
  • Comments should be insightful and substantive
  • Deliverable: Screenshots of original post with custom graphic and engagement screenshots

Blog Post Creation & Social Media Sharing (30 points)

Write 500-Word Blog Post (20 points)

  • Choose topic relevant to personal brand (career journey, industry trends, challenges and growth)
  • Use AI tools (ChatGPT, Jasper AI, Claude AI) to refine content for clarity and engagement

Design Social Media Post for Blog Promotion (10 points)

  • Create custom LinkedIn post graphic using Canva or Adobe Express
  • Include headline, key insight, or compelling quote from blog
  • Write 3-4 sentence LinkedIn caption summarizing blog and encouraging engagement
  • Deliverable: Link to published blog post and screenshot of LinkedIn promotion post

Class Participation (10 points)

  • Provide feedback on classmate's LinkedIn content, banner, or blog topics
  • Share insights on social media branding learnings
  • Assessment: Based on weekly engagement and contribution quality

Week 11: Portfolio Website Development (Total: 100 Points)

Contribution Opportunity (20 points)

  • Actively participate in website development troubleshooting sessions and design critiques
  • Share Vercel v0 tips, deployment solutions, and design best practices with classmates
  • Provide constructive feedback during wireframe presentations and website reviews
  • Deliverable: Documentation of participation in development sessions and quality feedback provided to peers

Research of Tools/Data Sources (20 points)

  • Research Vercel v0 capabilities, deployment options, and integration possibilities with marketing automation tools
  • Investigate best practices for portfolio websites in marketing technology and automation fields
  • Analyze competitor portfolios and identify effective design patterns for showcasing technical projects
  • Deliverable: Comprehensive research documentation including Vercel v0 capabilities analysis and competitor portfolio review

AI + Website Integration (20 points)

  • Use AI tools to generate website copy, optimize content structure, and refine messaging
  • Create AI-powered contact forms or visitor engagement features using Vercel's capabilities
  • Design automated workflows for website analytics, visitor tracking, or content updates
  • Deliverable: AI-generated website content with optimization documentation and automated website engagement workflows

Website Development & Wireframing (20 points)

  • Create comprehensive wireframes in Figma (homepage, portfolio section, about page, contact page)
  • Build and deploy functional website using Vercel v0 based on wireframes
  • Include responsive design elements and professional styling
  • Deliverable: Complete Figma wireframe set + deployed Vercel v0 website + documentation of build process

Peer Review (10 points)

  • Review assigned classmates' wireframes and provide UX/design feedback
  • Test peers' deployed websites for functionality, user experience, and mobile responsiveness
  • Deliverable: Detailed peer reviews focusing on wireframe quality and website functionality

Documentation & Organization (10 points)

  • Create professional development portfolio documenting entire website creation process
  • Include wireframe iterations, design decisions, technical challenges, and solutions
  • Write 200-word reflection on development process and lessons learned
  • Deliverable: Comprehensive website development portfolio with process documentation and reflection
