📌 Course Overview

Welcome to INFO7375: Branding & AI! This course explores the intersection of branding and artificial intelligence, teaching you to create a compelling professional identity while mastering cutting-edge AI tools and automation frameworks.

Two Parallel Tracks:

  • Weeks 1-6: Madison Framework - Learn and extend the open-source, agent-based AI marketing intelligence framework
  • Weeks 7-12: Personal Brand Development - Create your professional brand using AI tools

Key Focus Areas:

  • Building a strong personal brand identity that stands out
  • Mastering AI tools for content creation and automation
  • Creating portfolio pieces that showcase technical and creative skills
  • Developing professional materials that work effectively in today's job market

🎯 Assignment 1 Steps

Part 1️⃣: Course Onboarding (10 points)

A. Professional Introduction (5 points)

Your Task:
🔹 Create a professional introduction including:

  • Current background and experience
  • Career aspirations (2-3 year vision)
  • Why you're interested in AI and branding
  • One unique perspective or skill you bring
  • 3-5 relevant skills or technologies you work with

🔹 Format for Figma:

  • Design a visual introduction card
  • Include professional photo or avatar
  • Make it visually engaging and memorable
  • Balance creativity with clarity

💡 Pro Tip: Include both technical terms and plain language descriptions of your skills (e.g., "Machine Learning (ML)" not just "ML") - this helps your professional materials work better everywhere.

Deliverable: 📍 Professional introduction on your Figma board. Sign up for 3 free Figma boards using your NEU email address. https://www.figma.com/figjam/

B. Skills & Development Goals Inventory (5 points)

Your Task:
🔹 Create a skills assessment visualization showing:

Current Skills (rate yourself 1-5):

  • AI Tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, etc.)
  • Design & Branding (visual design, brand strategy)
  • Technical Skills (programming, web development, data)
  • Professional Skills (writing, presentation, project management)

Market Awareness:

  • Research 3-5 job postings in your target field
  • Note which skills appear most frequently
  • Identify both technical requirements and soft skills
  • Consider emerging technologies and trends

🔹 Identify 3 areas you want to strengthen this semester
🔹 Explain why these areas matter for your career goals

Deliverable: 📍 Skills inventory visualization with improvement areas on Figma

Part 2️⃣: Madison Framework Exploration (5 points)

Your Task:
🔹 Explore the Madison Framework GitHub repository:

  • Review the README and documentation https://github.com/Humanitariansai/Madison
  • Examine existing components and examples
  • Think about real-world applications

🔹 Choose your semester project focus:

  • Select a component to extend or improve
  • Consider what skills this will help you develop
  • Think about how to describe this project professionally

🔹 Write a brief proposal (150-200 words):

  • What will you work on?
  • Why is this valuable to the framework?
  • What skills will you gain or demonstrate?
  • How might this apply to real marketing/branding challenges?

💡 Portfolio Thinking: Use action verbs (developed, implemented, designed) when describing your project - this will help when adding it to your resume later

Deliverable: 📍 Madison framework project proposal on Figma board

Part 3️⃣: Personal Brand Baseline & Goals (5 points)

Your Task:
🔹 Current State Assessment:

  • Screenshot your current LinkedIn profile
  • Note any existing portfolio or personal website
  • Document your current professional story
  • Identify areas for enhancement

🔹 Professional Standards Awareness:

  • Understand that modern resumes need to work for both humans and ATS systems
  • Recognize that portfolios should be both creative and demonstrate technical skills
  • Note the importance of consistent professional presence across all platforms
  • Consider how keywords from job postings align with your experience

🔹 Define Semester Goals:

  • Technical Goal: What Madison framework contribution will you make?
  • Brand Goal: What personal brand outcome do you want?
  • Portfolio Goal: What will showcase your capabilities?
  • Network Goal: Who do you want to connect with?

Deliverable: 📍 Baseline assessment and goals section on Figma board

🚀 Success Tips

💡 For Your Introduction:

  • Show your personality while maintaining professionalism
  • Make it visually memorable
  • Include relevant skills naturally
  • Tell your story authentically

💡 For Skills Assessment:

  • Be honest about current levels
  • Look at actual job postings for guidance
  • Focus on both technical and soft skills
  • Plan realistic growth paths

💡 For Professional Materials:

  • Quality content is always most important
  • Clear communication beats keyword stuffing
  • Consistency across platforms builds credibility
  • Both human appeal and searchability matter

💡 For Your Brand:

  • Authenticity resonates with people
  • Clarity helps in all contexts
  • Your unique perspective is valuable
  • Professional doesn't mean boring
