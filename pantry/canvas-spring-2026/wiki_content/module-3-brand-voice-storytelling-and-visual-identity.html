📅 Module 3 Overview
Total Points: 450

Module Objectives

Develop a comprehensive personal brand through strategic storytelling, voice development, and AI-powered visual content creation. Students will craft compelling narratives, optimize their digital presence across platforms, and create cohesive visual identities that reinforce their professional expertise.

Learning Objectives

✅ Shape a compelling brand voice using AI writing tools and storytelling frameworks
✅ Develop and refine LinkedIn brand presence with optimized profiles and engagement
✅ Create AI-generated visual content that aligns with personal brand identity
✅ Integrate Jungian brand archetypes into comprehensive branding strategies
✅ Build thought leadership through blog content and social media engagement
✅ Analyze and select optimal portfolio website platforms for brand showcase

Content Outline and Assignments

Brand Voice & Storytelling Development (40 points)

  • Brand Voice Definition (10 points): Create 2-3 sentence brand voice statement using AI tools
  • Personal Brand Storytelling (20 points): Develop 300-word brand story using storytelling arcs (Hero's Journey, Save the Cat, Rags to Riches, Overcoming the Monster, The Quest)
  • AI-Generated Content Refinement (10 points): Submit AI draft and refined final version with explanation of changes

LinkedIn Brand Development (30 points)

  • Profile Optimization (20 points): Craft LinkedIn headline and About section (150+ words) using UVP and brand pillars
  • Engagement & Thought Leadership (10 points): Share project updates and engage with 3+ industry posts (submit screenshots)

Blog Content & Website Integration (30 points)

  • Blog Post Creation (20 points): Write and publish 500-word blog post on portfolio website using AI tools
  • LinkedIn Blog Sharing (10 points): Share blog post on LinkedIn with strategic caption (submit screenshot)

Portfolio Platform Analysis (20 points)

  • Platform Research & Comparison: Analyze Wix, Adobe Portfolio, Squarespace, Vercel, Webflow, Google Scholar, ResearchGate
  • Deliverable: 300-word comparison report with platform recommendation

AI-Generated Visuals & Brand Refinement (100 points)

  • AI Image Generation (40 points): Create 3 brand-aligned images using MidJourney, DALL·E, Canva AI, Adobe Firefly, or Stable Diffusion with 150-word reflection
  • Visual Content Refinement (40 points): Submit original and refined versions with explanation of improvements
  • Class Participation (20 points): Active engagement in discussions and guest speaker Q&As

Jungian Archetypes & Social Media Branding (100 points)

  • Jungian Brand Archetype Exercise (40 points): Create 1-2 AI-generated images reflecting your brand archetype with 200-word analysis
  • LinkedIn Visual Optimization (30 points): Create/refine profile picture and banner with before-and-after comparison
  • Social Media Thought Leadership (25 points): Share AI-generated visual on LinkedIn with 100-150 word caption, engage with 3+ industry posts
  • Class Participation & Engagement (5 points): Continued active involvement in class discussions

Workshop Participation (130 points total)

  • Brand Storytelling Workshop (10 points)
  • LinkedIn Branding Challenge (10 points)
  • Portfolio Blog Review (10 points)
  • Visual Branding Workshop (10 points)
  • AI Content Creation Lab (10 points)
  • Active class participation across all activities (80 points)
