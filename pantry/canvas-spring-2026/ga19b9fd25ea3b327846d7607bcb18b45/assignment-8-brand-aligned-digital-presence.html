Building Your Live Website, Resumes & LinkedIn Header

Due: March 27, 2026 at 11:59 PM | Total Points: 100 (80 core + 20 quality)

Overview

Your brand strategy is locked. Your visual identity is defined. Now you build the actual thing. This assignment is about implementation — taking everything from Assignments 6 and 7 and creating a live digital presence. First version, not final version. Functional over flawless. Ship it.

The only required documentation piece is your color accessibility check. Everything else is about building and launching.

Part 1: Brand-Aligned Website — First Version (30 Points)

Website Draft (25 Points)

Choose one platform and build a responsive first draft of your brand website:

  • Vercel v0.dev
  • Framer AI
  • Wix AI
  • Squarespace AI
  • Other AI-powered platform (instructor approval required)

Your site must implement your Assignment 7 brand assets — logo, color palette, typography system, and visual style. No blank placeholder boxes where your brand should live.

Required Sections:

  • Hero: Your name or brand name, professional role or value proposition, compelling call-to-action
  • Projects/Products: Minimum 3 projects or offerings with titles, images, and descriptions
  • About: Personal story or company narrative
  • Contact: Professional contact information and a clear action for visitors to take
  • Responsive Layout: Test it on your phone. If it's broken on mobile, it's not done.

Deliverable: Live website link

Color Accessibility Check (5 Points)

Test your text-background color combinations using WebAIM (https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/) or a comparable tool. Screenshot your results and submit as a PDF. Accessibility isn't optional.

Deliverable: PDF with accessibility check screenshots

Part 2: Visual Brand Extensions (20 Points)

LinkedIn Header — First Version (10 Points)

Create a LinkedIn header image using AI tools (Canva AI, DALL·E, MidJourney, Adobe Firefly, etc.) that:

  • Follows your established brand system — colors, typography, visual elements
  • Is optimized for LinkedIn header dimensions: 1584 × 396 pixels
  • Includes your value proposition or tagline
  • Works visually alongside your profile picture

Keep it clean. Headers with too much text get ignored.

Deliverable: LinkedIn header image (PNG or JPG) in a PDF

AI-Generated Brand Visuals (10 Points)

Use an AI image generation tool to create 3 brand-aligned visuals for your website. Choose from MidJourney, DALL·E, Canva AI, Adobe Firefly, Stable Diffusion, or similar.

Your images should represent your expertise, industry, values, or your startup's offering and mission. Visual consistency across all three matters — they should feel like they belong to the same brand family.

Deliverable: Three AI-generated brand images in a PDF

Part 3: Brand-Aligned Professional Documents (30 Points)

ATS-Friendly Resume — First Version (15 Points)

Build an ATS-optimized resume that still carries your brand's DNA:

  • Single-column layout for clean parsing
  • Strategic keyword implementation for your target roles or industry
  • Subtle brand elements (colors, fonts, logo placement) that don't interfere with ATS scanning
  • Submit in both PDF and Word/text formats

ATS-friendly doesn't mean boring — it means smart. Branding lives in the details.

Deliverable: ATS-optimized resume (PDF + Word)

Visually Designed Resume — First Version (15 Points)

Create a visually distinctive resume that expresses your full brand system:

  • Research Pinterest for design inspiration before you start
  • Incorporate your professional headshot or brand image
  • Implement your complete visual system: colors, typography, layout hierarchy
  • Information should be easy to scan in 6 seconds

Deliverable: Visually designed resume (PDF)
