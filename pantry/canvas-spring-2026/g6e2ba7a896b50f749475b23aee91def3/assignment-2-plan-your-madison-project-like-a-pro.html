Part 3: Your PRD (Product Requirements Document) (40 points)

"Write Like a Product Manager"

Create a PRD on your Figma board with these sections:

Problem Statement (10 pts)

  • What specific problem does your target company/role face?
  • Who experiences this problem?
  • What's the cost of not solving it?

Proposed Solution (10 pts)

  • Your Madison-powered approach
  • Why Madison vs. other solutions?
  • What makes this technically interesting?

User Stories (10 pts)

Write 3 in this format:

  • "As a [role], I want [feature] so that [benefit]"
  • Example: "As a Frontend Developer, I want automated accessibility checking so that I catch issues before PR review"

Success Metrics (10 pts)

  • What improves? By how much?
  • Time saved? Errors reduced? Cost cut?
  • How would you measure success?

✅ Completion (32 pts): All sections present with clear content
⭐ Excellence (8 pts):

  • Used actual metrics from industry research
  • Referenced specific tools/APIs you'll integrate
  • Included edge cases and failure modes
  • Format/presentation is professional

Nina's Example: "My PRD for JungAI includes success metrics from real brand consistency studies showing 73% of brands fail to maintain voice across channels"
