# Branding and AI
## Compact Tik TOC Architecture

**Book type:** Course textbook / practitioner handbook  
**Status:** Retrospective architecture for a developed manuscript  

## Core Thesis

This book teaches brand strategy in an AI-mediated world: how to use AI for research, positioning, voice, content, and creative exploration while preserving strategic judgment, authenticity, evidence, and brand coherence.

## Primary Reader

MBA students, marketing students, founders, strategists, creators, and communications professionals who need practical brand work with AI support.

## Learning Arc

1. **Brand foundations:** audience, promise, positioning, differentiation, trust, and voice.
2. **AI-assisted brand work:** audits, personas, competitive landscapes, copy, visuals, and campaigns.
3. **Strategic governance:** consistency, ethics, disclosure, measurement, and long-term brand equity.

## Chapter Architecture

Chapters should begin with a brand decision, not an AI tool. AI enters as research assistant, pattern finder, draft generator, or critique partner after the strategic question is clear.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can conduct an AI-assisted brand audit, produce a positioning statement, build a voice guide, and design a coherent campaign with human strategic accountability.

## Adoption Risks

- AI examples can date quickly.
- Brand theory must not disappear under tool demos.
- Generated personas and market claims require evidence checks.

## Production Notes

Use Tik TOC to align chapters to a final brand strategy package.
